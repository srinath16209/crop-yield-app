Welcome to the Smart Crop Yield Prediction System — a platform designed to empower farmers, researchers, and agricultural planners with accurate insights into crop performance.Using advanced machine learning models such as Random Forest, XGBoost, CatBoost, and Artificial Neural Networks, our system analyzes soil nutrients, weather conditions, and farming practices to predict yields with precision. Agriculture is the backbone of our economy, and making informed decisions about crop planning, fertilizer use, and irrigation can significantly improve productivity. This tool helps bridge the gap between data and sustainable farming.
